 E-Commerce Sales Analysis | Power BI Dashboard

## Business problem
An e-commerce business needed visibility into which months, categories, and brands were driving sales — and which were underperforming — to guide inventory and marketing decisions.

## What I built
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 12 months of order-level sales data across product categories, brands, and customer regions. The dashboard includes slicers for dynamic filtering by month, category, and brand.

## Key findings
- **July** recorded the highest revenue — suggesting a seasonal demand spike worth targeting with promotions
- **March** had the highest order volume despite lower average order value — indicating high-frequency, low-ticket purchases
- **Electronics and Fashion** together accounted for the majority of total sales across all months
- **Xiaomi** ranked as the top brand by revenue, outperforming competitors across multiple categories

## Business recommendation
Inventory planning should prioritize Electronics and Fashion 6–8 weeks before July. March's high order volume at low value suggests a bundling or upsell opportunity.

## Tools used
Power BI · DAX · Microsoft Excel · Power Query
